What is Ducklings?

Ducklings is an addictive endless jumping arcade game for everyone that seeks a break from daily life. You are controlling a little duck on its journey on a never ending river. Jump over obstacles and avoid falling down the waterfalls to get as far as you can. It’s all about immediate reaction and precise jumping skills. How far can you get?

Features

  • Cute duckling as the main character
  • Precise jumping physics
  • Randomly generated endless river world in 3D
  • Unlock 15 beautiful destinations to discover
  • Game Center Leaderboard

About SNake Oil Studios

Snake Oil Studios is a brand new Games Development Studio in the heart of Berlin. It was founded by Johan Dettmar and Sven Schmidt in October 2015. The endless jumping arcade game Ducklings is the first game of Snake Oil Studios.
